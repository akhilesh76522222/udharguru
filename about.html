About UdharGuru

UdharGuru is a simple and fast ledger app designed to help shop owners track credits (You Got) and debits (You Gave) with ease. Our goal is to save time, reduce mistakes, and provide clear statements.

Features include adding transactions, voice entry, generating PDFs, and sharing summaries. We respect user privacy and never access your data without permission.
